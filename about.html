My daily goals:

#1. Make my team faster and the infrastructure more reliable.

Things I make are meant to be SIMPLE and reproducible. This is usually a good first step to achieve reliability. I'm always able to quickly explain any change in the infrastructure, the code or the design to my colleagues (and yes, project managers are also colleagues).

#2. Make business goals my goals.

I am results oriented. New technology? fun I guess. Getting MEANINGFUL outcomes in the projects? Things just got real. I’m not an advocate of anything until I hear what the problem is. People over Process over Tools.

#3. Be open. Be mindful. Be communicative.

Team, team, team (much like Denholm Reynholm from the IT crowd). Communication and collaboration should be the standard on a healthy team. Among other things, I believe in being open to everyone on a team. Code reviews, constant feedback & getting involved.

I'm an AWS expert and I have a MSc in Machine Learning.
